Recalled Out Summer

Previous Movie

Shiki Ryougi, Mikiya Kokutou, and Touko Aozaki begin investigating a bomber after they witness a nearby explosion. That very same night, Shiki catches a glimpse of the bomber, and as a result, he becomes fixated on her. To get rid of her, the man plays a game of cat and mouse in attempts to lure her to an empty parking garage. And bombs are not the only thing he has in his arsenal: he also has the ability to see the future, and he intends to bring an end to Shiki.

Elsewhere, a few days before the bombing, a student at Reien Girls' Academy, Shizune Seo, plans to head home for the summer. However, while exiting a bus, she suddenly has a vision of the future involving a nearby stranger's death. While trying to warn the stranger, she meets Mikiya, who succeeds in utilizing Shizune's information.

Subsequently, an employee is sent on a job with his employer's 10-year-old daughter in tow. However, the subject of his investigation turns out to be a ghost from both of their pasts.

This movie takes place during the main time-line of the series, while also taking place in the future.
