Who we are……

​

3DAssist Tallaght is a registered charity based in TU Dublin made up of students, past students and staff.​

​ We were set up in December 2014 with a view to 3D printing prosthetic hands and arms for people who need them. ​ ​

We are the Irish chapter of the worldwide E-Nable organisation. ​We are the Irish Chapter of e-NABLE.​

​ There are 7000 members of e-NABLE ​ 2000 devices created and gifted to individuals​ in over 45 countries.​
