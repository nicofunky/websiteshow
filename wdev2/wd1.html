Web Development 1

Presenting,
The main Web Development Project. Lead by Nina Lyons.

The project consisted of creating a website about a movie, movie series, or director of your choice. I chose mine to be off a movie series that I liked.

View the website here.

Do keep in mind a button has been added to the top right of all the pages. It's been added to make navigating back to this page easy.

The page count is low (12) as the project brief stated that our website cannot have more than 12 pages. It's a sensible limitation to prevent students from going overboard with their pages. However, in this case it made my website more barren than it would have been originally.
