Mikiya notices Shiki at his high school entrance ceremony, not for the first time, and they become acquaintances through rooftop lunchtime conversations. As Shiki begins opening up to him, Mikiya learns about her unique upbringing and circumstance.

Meanwhile, a series of mysterious murders take place across Mifune City. A large scale police investigation is started. Because of his cousin's work as a police investigator, Mikiya is given insight into the investigation. Concerned for Shiki's safety, Mikiya decides to watch over her, but while doing so, he makes an absolutely frightening discovery that changed his life forever.
