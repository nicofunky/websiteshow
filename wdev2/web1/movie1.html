In September 1998, a series of random suicides leaves Japan baffled and confused. A detective agency specializing in paranormal occurrences picks up on a few similarities while tying to solve the case:
All the victims are schoolgirls, and all of them have jumped to their deaths from atop the ancient Fujou Building, a skyscraper that is scheduled to be bulldozed soon.

The head of the agency, Touko Aozaki, sends Mikiya Kokutou and Shiki Ryougi to investigate. The kind and surprisingly normal Mikiya serves as a contrast to the cold and mysterious Shiki, who possesses the magical ability "Mystic Eyes of Death Perception" which allows her to see, and bring, an end to all things.
