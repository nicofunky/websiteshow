WHO ARE WE? WHY THIS WEBSITE?

We are a group of people that enjoy Dublin Castle!

This website became an idea that our group came up with during one of our many coffee breaks at our dayjobs. There was simply not enough appreciation for one of the central parts of county Dublin and even Ireland. Therefore, this website was created to help alleviate this issue. We are passionate for this castle. We are so dedicated that we would marry it if it was a person. We love this historical artefact this much.
