CASTLE STATISTICS!

Dublin Castle is a castle! It has walls and rooms. John Cornforth described the Castle as "not even a work of architecture" but "a piece of English make-do and mend".

It has several standing towers. Bermingham Tower, Record Tower, Bedford Tower, Powder Tower and Cork Tower.

Most of the rooms inside the castle are accessible by tours.
